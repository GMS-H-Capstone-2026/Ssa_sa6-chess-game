How to Play & Controls

Basic Interactions

1. Drag & Drop: Drag any piece and drop it on a highlighted square to play a move.

2. Click to Move: Alternatively, click a piece to view valid moves, then click the target square to move it.

Special Moves

  • Castling: If the King and Rook haven't moved and the path is clear, move the King two squares toward the Rook. The Rook jumps over automatically.
  • En Passant: If an opponent pawn advances two squares past yours, you can capture it diagonally on your very next turn.
  • Promotion: When a pawn reaches the opposite edge of the board, it automatically promotes. Select your preferred piece (Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight) in the pop-up menu.

AI Difficulty Info

  • Easy: plays quickly and makes frequent positional errors.
  • Medium: analyzes 2 steps ahead. Employs tactical play.
  • Hard: analyzes 3-4 steps ahead. Strongly defends positions and punishes errors.
